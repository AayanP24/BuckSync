Privacy Policy for BuckSync

This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and disclose your information when you use the BuckSync mobile application (the "App"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience when using our App.

1. Information We Collect
A. Information You Provide Directly:

We collect information that you voluntarily provide when you use the App. This includes:

Account Information: When you create an account, we collect your phone number (used for authentication and group linking) and your chosen username (visible to other group members).

Profile Data: When you complete your profile, we may collect your name.

Transaction and Expense Data: When you add an expense, we collect the description, amount, who paid, and who is included in the split. We also track balances within your groups.

Contact Information: To use our "Add with 1 tap" feature, you may choose to provide access to your phone’s contact list. We only access this information to identify existing users and invite new ones at your explicit request.

B. Information We Collect Automatically:

When you use the App, we may automatically collect certain information about your device and usage, including:

Device Information: We may collect your device’s unique identifier (UUID), operating system, and hardware model for troubleshooting and app performance analysis.

Usage Data: We collect information about how you interact with the App, such as the features you use and the frequency of your use, to help us improve the App.

2. How We Use Your Information
We use the collected information for the following purposes:

To Provide and Maintain the App: To manage your account, process transactions, calculate balances, and enable smart settlements.

To Facilitate Social Interaction: To connect you with other users in your group, show you who owes whom, and send gentle reminders (notifications).

To Improve Our Services: To analyze usage patterns and fix bugs, enhancing your user experience.

To Send Personal Notifications: To send individual alerts related to your account and group balances (e.g., "New expense added by Ahmed," "You have a pending balance").

To Handle Support and Communications: To respond to your comments, questions, and support requests.

3. How We Share Your Information
We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information in the following ways:

With Other Group Members: The primary goal of the App is shared expense tracking. Members of your groups can see your username, phone number (if you are in their contacts), added expenses, and balances.

With Service Providers: We may share your information with trusted third-party service providers (like Firebase for authentication and database management) who assist us in operating our App. These providers have access to your information only to perform specific tasks on our behalf and are obligated not to disclose or use it for any other purpose.

For Legal Reasons: We may disclose your information if required to do so by law or in response to a valid request by a public authority (e.g., a court or government agency).

In Connection with a Business Transfer: If we are involved in a merger, acquisition, or asset sale, your information may be transferred as a business asset.

4. Your Choices and Data Rights
You have certain choices and rights regarding your personal information:

Access and Update Your Data: You can access and update your account and profile information directly within the App settings.

Opt-Out of Notifications: You can manage your notification preferences (like turning off group spam) within your device settings.

Request Data Deletion: You can request that we delete your account and associated personal information. This can be done through the App or by contacting us at the support email provided below. Please note that some information may be retained for legal or administrative purposes.

5. Security
We take reasonable measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, loss, misuse, or alteration. However, please be aware that no method of transmission over the internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

6. Children's Privacy
The App is not intended for children under the age of 4. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under this age. If we become aware that we have inadvertently collected personal information from a child, we will take steps to delete it.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy in the App and on our GitHub repository. We encourage you to review this page periodically for any updates.
