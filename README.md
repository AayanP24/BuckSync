# BuckSync
Split expenses effortlessly with SettleUp. The drama-free way to manage group costs, from trips to lunch. No manual notes or awkward reminders!
Suggested Text:
Ditch the manual tracking and awkward money talks! BuckSync makes splitting expenses clear, automatic, and stress-free.

Features:

EASY GROUP CREATION: Start a group for "Goa Trip," "Lunch Crew," or "Roommates" and invite friends in seconds.

QUICK EXPENSE ENTRY: Enter costs in any currency (like AED), select who paid, and instantly choose who's splitting.

SMART SPLITTING OPTIONS: Split equally, or customize percentages and amounts for those "one person didn't eat" scenarios.

PERSONALIZED BALANCE TRACKING: Forget "A owes B, B owes C..." SettleUp shows you exactly your net perspective: "You owe Ahmed 35 AED" or "Sarah owes you 10 AED."

SMART SETTLEMENTS: Our optimized payment chain simplifies complex debts into a single, straightforward payment.

GENTLE, NEUTRAL REMINDERS: SettleUp sends professional, automated notifications, eliminating the awkwardness of manual debt collection.

Remove the friction from shared finances. Download BuckSync and keep your relationships drama-free.
